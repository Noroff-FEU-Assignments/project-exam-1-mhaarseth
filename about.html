I have got a lot of ideas for titles laying around. a whisper light and low is a space where all these become texts, accompanied by an AI's interpretation of that line.

As an example, the image on this particular page is one of DALL-E's interpretations of the title of this blog.

The goal is to create a space and a reason to write more, and more often. For the time being it is all in english, but this is purely due to this intially being an exam project. It will change after christmas.

The layout is supposed to somewhat resemble a magazine, and is meant to be simple and effective, focusing on the texts.

The prose on the page is written especially for this project, in a rush, and is in no way meant to represent my writings as a whole.
